Our story

Born from a grandmother's kitchen

It all began with a cast-iron pan, a worn recipe card, and the smell of Sunday mornings. Our founder, Maria, grew up watching her grandmother pour batter with quiet precision — never measuring, always perfect. In 2015, she turned those memories into Fluffies, a place where every pancake carries that same unhurried care.


We source locally, grind our own flour, and hand-pick every seasonal fruit. No shortcuts, no compromises — just the honest pleasure of a beautifully made pancake.

The craft

Every layer, intentional

We don't rush the batter. We rest it overnight so the gluten relaxes and the flavors deepen. Our griddles are seasoned for years, and our chefs know the sound a perfect pancake makes when it's ready to flip. This is slow food done right.


From classic buttermilk to exotic matcha-honey, every creation on our menu has been tested hundreds of times before it earns a place in your morning.

2015

The First Stack

Maria opened our flagship location in a tiny corner shop, serving just three flavors and selling out every day by noon.

2018

Growing Fluffy

Expanded to five locations and launched our weekend farmers-market pop-ups, bringing fresh-battered pancakes to the streets.

2024

A National Name

Named "Best Brunch Spot" by three regional food magazines and shipped our pancake mix to over 40 countries worldwide.

About us

More than breakfast — it's a ritual

We believe mornings deserve intention. Fluffies is built on the idea that the first meal of the day should slow you down, not speed you up. Every location is designed to feel like a warm kitchen, not a fast-food counter.

Our team is made up of passionate chefs, local farmers, and people who genuinely believe that a good pancake can change your mood, your morning, and maybe your whole day.
